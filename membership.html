Membership at COV

At COV, membership is more than a credential—it's a commitment to excellence, integrity, and lifelong learning in the valuation profession. Whether you're a student, an emerging practitioner, or a seasoned expert, we offer a pathway tailored to your stage of professional growth.

Membership Categories

COV's membership structure is designed to support you at every stage of your professional journey.

Student Member

Ideal for current students or recent graduates pursuing academic programmes related to valuation, finance, economics, law, engineering, or real estate.

  • Provides early exposure to the professional valuation ecosystem
  • Access to foundational learning resources, events, and mentorship
  • Prepares members for transition into Affiliate or Chartered levels
  • Encourages participation in student chapters, competitions, and forums
Register Now

Affiliate Member

Suited for early-career professionals or individuals transitioning into valuation, who have foundational knowledge or a demonstrated interest in the profession.

  • Ideal for those seeking structured pathways toward becoming Chartered Members or Registered Valuers
  • Access to foundational CPD programmes, industry events, and mentorship
  • Serves as a launchpad for professional certification and practical exposure
  • Sub-categories include:
    • Affiliate (Land & Building Valuation)
    • Affiliate (Plant & Machinery Valuation)
    • Affiliate (Business Valuation)
    • Affiliate (Jewellery & Precious Assets)
    • Affiliate (Financial Instruments & Securities)
Register Now

Chartered Member

This is the core professional grade of membership, awarded to those who meet rigorous standards of competency, ethics, and practical experience.

  • Requires formal qualification and experience in valuation or allied domains
  • Recognised through post-nominal: CM-COV
  • Eligible for advanced CPD, technical committees, and mentoring roles
  • Demonstrates commitment to international valuation standards and ethical practice
Register Now

Fellow Member

Reserved for accomplished professionals with substantial experience, contributions to the valuation community, and a reputation for integrity and leadership.

  • Recognised through post-nominal: FM-COV
  • Eligible to chair committees, lead technical discussions, and represent COV in global forums
  • Mentors junior members and contributes to setting benchmarks for the profession
  • Acknowledged for research, innovation, or long-standing impact in valuation practice
Register Now

Institutional Member

Open to valuation firms, academic institutions, industry bodies, and organisations engaged in or supporting the valuation profession.

  • Encourages organisational participation in COV's professional ecosystem
  • Access to team-wide CPD opportunities, joint events, and research partnerships
  • Opportunity to contribute to capacity building, policy advocacy, and knowledge sharing
  • Demonstrates institutional commitment to ethical and global best practices in valuation

Student Member – Eligibility Criteria

Asset Class Education Experience
Plant, Equipment & Infrastructure Undergraduate or student pursuing Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Communication, Electronics & Instrumentation, Production, Chemical, Textiles, Leather, Metallurgy, or Aeronautical Engineering. No experience required
Land and Building Undergraduate or student pursuing Civil Engineering, Architecture, or Town Planning. No experience required
Business Valuation Undergraduate or student pursuing Finance, Economics, or Business Administration. No experience required
Financial Instruments Undergraduate or student pursuing Finance, Economics, or Accounting. No experience required

Affiliate Member – Eligibility Criteria

Asset Class Education Experience
Plant, Equipment & Infrastructure Graduate or Diploma in Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Communication, Electronics & Instrumentation, Production, Chemical, Textiles, Leather, Metallurgy, or Aeronautical Engineering. No experience required
Land and Building Graduate or Diploma in Civil Engineering, Architecture, or Town Planning. No experience required
Business Valuation Graduate or Diploma in Finance, Economics, or Business Administration. No experience required
Financial Instruments Graduate or Diploma in Finance, Economics, or Accounting. No experience required

Chartered Member – Eligibility Criteria

Asset Class Education Experience
Plant, Equipment & Infrastructure Graduate in Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Communication, Electronics & Instrumentation, Production, Chemical, Textiles, Leather, Metallurgy, or Aeronautical Engineering, OR Postgraduate in these fields or Valuation of Plant and Machinery. Minimum of 2 years in valuation; 1 year with postgraduate degree.
Land and Building Graduate in Civil Engineering, Architecture, or Town Planning, OR Postgraduate in these fields or Real Estate Valuation. No experience required
Business Valuation Member of ICAI, ICSI, or ICWAI, OR MBA (Finance), Graduate in Finance, Economics, or Business Administration, OR Postgraduate in these fields or Business Valuation. No experience required
Financial Instruments Member of ICAI, ICSI, or ICWAI, OR MBA (Finance), OR Postgraduate in Finance or Financial Instruments. No experience required

Fellow Member – Eligibility Criteria

Asset Class Education Experience
Plant, Equipment & Infrastructure Advanced degree in relevant engineering disciplines or Valuation of Plant and Machinery OR recognized expertise in the field. Associate Member in Plant, Equipment & Infrastructure who has been in continuous practice in India for at least five years
Land and Building Advanced degree in Civil Engineering, Architecture, Town Planning, or Real Estate Valuation OR recognized expertise Associate Member in Land and Building who has been in continuous practice in India for at least five years
Business Valuation Member of ICAI, ICSI, or ICWAI, OR MBA (Finance), Advanced degree in Finance, Economics, Business Administration, or Business Valuation OR recognized expertise in the field Associate Member in Business Valuation who has been in continuous practice in India for at least five years
Financial Instruments Member of ICAI, ICSI, or ICWAI, OR MBA (Finance), OR Postgraduate in Finance or Financial Instruments. Associate Member in Financial Instruments who has been in continuous practice in India for at least five years

Corporate & Institutional Member – Eligibility Criteria

Valuation firms, institutions, and organizations aligned with the Institution’s goals and the Membership committee will issue Corporate and Institution Membership case to case.
