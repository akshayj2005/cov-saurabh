Membership at COV

At COV, membership is more than a credential—it's a commitment to excellence, integrity, and lifelong learning in the valuation profession. Whether you're a student, an emerging practitioner, or a seasoned expert, we offer a pathway tailored to your stage of professional growth.

Affiliate Member

Where Learning Transforms into Practice

The Affiliate Membership is tailored for early-career professionals or individuals transitioning into the valuation profession. It bridges academic learning and practical exposure—enabling aspiring valuers to build a credible foundation in their chosen asset class.
This category is ideal for those seeking to establish competence, clarity, and professional alignment on their pathway to Chartered Membership or Registered Valuer status.

    Why Become an Affiliate Member?
  • A structured and recognised entry point into professional valuation.
  • Access to CPD programmes, industry-ready training, and expert mentorship.
  • Exposure to hands-on valuation concepts, case studies, and knowledge-sharing forums.
  • Build your confidence, network, and capability for regulatory and global opportunities.

    • Affiliate (Plant & Machinery Valuation)
    • Affiliate (Business Valuation)
    • Affiliate (Jewellery & Precious Assets)

Chartered Member

Professional Recognition. Global Credibility

Chartered Membership represents the core professional grade at COV — designed for individuals who demonstrate a high level of competence, ethical commitment, and applied experience in valuation. It signifies a professional milestone, offering credibility, recognition, and pathways for leadership in the valuation community.

    Why Become a Chartered Member?
  • Earn the CM-COV Post-Nominal: A mark of distinction in the valuation profession.
  • Recognised Professional Status: Shows you’ve met rigorous education, experience, and ethical benchmarks.
  • Access to Premium Opportunities: Eligible for technical committees, advanced CPD, thought-leadership forums, and mentoring roles.
  • Global Readiness: Demonstrates alignment with international standards and ethical valuation practice.

Fellow Member

Where Leadership Meets Legacy

The Fellow Membership (FM COV) is the highest recognition awarded by the Council of Valuers, reserved for seasoned professionals who have demonstrated exceptional expertise, leadership, and long-standing contributions to the valuation ecosystem.
Fellow Members are acknowledged not just for their experience, but for their enduring impact—through mentorship, research, governance, or thought leadership—within the profession.

    Why Become a Fellow Member?
  • Prestigious recognition as a senior leader in the valuation profession.
  • Eligibility to chair committees, mentor future professionals, and represent COV globally.
  • Access to high-level consultations, expert panels, and policymaking dialogues.
  • Demonstrates your role in shaping standards, strategy, and the future of valuation in India and abroad.

Chartered Member – Eligibility Criteria

Asset Class Education Experience
Plant, Equipment & Infrastructure Graduate in Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Communication, Electronics & Instrumentation, Production, Chemical, Textiles, Leather, Metallurgy, or Aeronautical Engineering, OR Postgraduate in these fields or Valuation of Plant and Machinery. Minimum of 2 years in valuation; 1 year with postgraduate degree.
Land and Building Graduate in Civil Engineering, Architecture, or Town Planning, OR Postgraduate in these fields or Real Estate Valuation. No experience required

Financial Instruments Member of ICAI, ICSI, or ICWAI, OR MBA (Finance), OR Postgraduate in Finance or Financial Instruments. Associate Member in Financial Instruments who has been in continuous practice in India for at least five years
